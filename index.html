Epigraph

for the fall was a good fifteen feet,

but Mowgli fell as Baloo had taught him to fall,

and landed on his feet.”

― Rudyard Kipling, The Jungle Book

Preface

I chose to translate the Jungle Book because it was a book and movie

that I connected with in my childhood. I thought it would be fun to do on

this project. One of my most troubling experiences within this project was

learning how to do certain things in HTML that I didn't have any knowledge

on. Some of those things being how to format and change sizes. Even though they

were challenging, I came out this project knowing more than I came into it with.

Page 5 - Emoji Translation

💀 🥩 🍖 😁

He scuttled to the back of the cave, where he found the cone of a buck with some meat on it, and sat cracking the end merrily.

✊ 👅

"All thanks for this good meal," he said, licking his lips.

👑 ✨ 👧 👦 👁 👸

"How beautiful are the noble children! How large are their eyes! And so young too! Indeed, indeed, I might have remembered that the children of kings are men from the beginning."

👶 🐺 😬

Now, Tabaqui knew as well as any one else that there is nothing so unlucky as to compliment children to their faces; and it pleased him to see Mother and Father Wolf look uncomfortable.

🙂 🙄 🦁 🏞 ➡ 🌕

Tabaqui sat still, rejoicing in the mischief that he had made, and then he said spitefully: "Shere Khan, the Big One, has shifted his hunter-grounds. He will hunt among these hills during the next moon, so has told me."

🦁 🏞

Shere Khan was the tiger who lived near the Waingunga River, twenty miles away.
